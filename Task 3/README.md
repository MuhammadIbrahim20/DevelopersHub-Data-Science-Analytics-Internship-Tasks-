📉 Task 3: Customer Churn Prediction (Bank Customers)
The Task Objective: Successfully identify bank clients who are highly likely to close their accounts, providing actionable data for retention targeting.

Dropped uninformative identifier metrics to avoid overfitting. Encoded categorical features (Geography, Gender) using One-Hot and Label Encoding. Scaled continuous numeric elements and trained a supervised classification model, subsequently extracting and plotting structural feature importance ranks.

Results and Insights: Customer age and account balance are the primary driving metrics behind attrition risk. Older customer brackets and those with higher balance metrics show an elevated probability of churning.
