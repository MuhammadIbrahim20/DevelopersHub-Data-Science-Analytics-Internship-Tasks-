📞 Task 5: Personal Loan Acceptance Prediction
The Task Objective: Analyze direct telemarketing campaign customer groups to accurately predict which banking clients are likely to accept a personal loan offer.

Conducted structural EDA on core demographic variables like age, marital status, and job types. Implemented binary Logistic Regression and Decision Tree classifiers to segment receptive customer populations and extract clear business guidelines.

Results and Insights: Call duration and the outcome of historical marketing campaigns (poutcome) serve as high-converting features. Clients previously responsive to campaigns exhibit an exponentially higher likelihood of accepting new offers.
